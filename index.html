A bit about me

Beyond the day job.

Because you're not just hiring a skill set. You're getting a whole person.

I'm originally from Sydney, but London has been home for nine years now. Moving here was a lifelong aspiration. I grew up loving everything about British culture, the humour, rich music history, the vibrant energy, the sense that anything is possible. When I was offered a transfer opportunity, I didn't hesitate. The day I arrived, I knew this was where I was meant to be.

I'm based in East London, which is exactly the kind of place I thrive in: vibrant, diverse, and never boring. I love food, music, dancing and the arts. And I'm a mum of two young energetic boys, which keeps everything in perspective and reminds me why flexibility matters.

I live a healthy lifestyle where my family's wellbeing come first. That's a big part of why I've shifted away from full-time employment. I want to nourish my family and live a fulfilling life.

What drives me at work is the same principles as in my family life: I value people, relationships and live life with an emphasis on the human experience. Authenticity and integrity are paramount. I'm constantly evolving, picking up new skills, and finding new ways to stay interested and interesting. And I do my best to do all of this with a big smile and few jokes along the way!
